Our Story

Born on the Field

In 1965, a team of researchers at the University of Florida developed Gatorade to help the school's football team, the Gators, combat heat and dehydration during intense training and games. What started as a simple solution to a real problem has evolved into a global brand trusted by millions of athletes.

The original formula was created by Dr. Robert Cade and his team, who discovered that athletes needed more than just water to replace the fluids and electrolytes lost through sweat. This breakthrough in sports science revolutionized how athletes hydrate and perform.

Today, Gatorade continues to push the boundaries of sports science, developing new products and formulas that help athletes at every level achieve their best performance. From professional sports to weekend warriors, Gatorade is there when it matters most.

Our Mission

At Gatorade, our mission is simple: to fuel athletic performance and help athletes achieve their goals. We believe that proper hydration and nutrition are fundamental to success, whether you're competing for a championship or pushing your personal limits.

We're committed to advancing sports science through continuous research and innovation. Every product we create is backed by science and tested with real athletes in real conditions. We don't just make sports drinks—we engineer performance solutions.
